Catch the Tortoise

A 2-Player Tile Sorting Race

About the Game

Catch the Tortoise is a fast-paced sorting race inspired by Mahjong tiles! You and the bot each have a grid of face-down tiles arranged in 9 columns. On your turn, you push a blank space into the top of any column, which forces the bottom tile out. That tile gets flipped face-up and then needs to find its home.

If the revealed tile is your suit (Dots), you place it into its matching column number. If it belongs to the bot (Bamboo), it automatically goes to the bot's grid. The chain keeps going until a blank pops out, then you push it back in somewhere new. First player to get all their tiles sorted face-up in the right columns wins!

Quick Rules

  • Each grid has 9 columns with 4 face-down Mahjong tiles.
  • Push a blank into any column to pop the bottom tile out.
  • Dots tiles always go to your grid, Bamboo tiles go to the bot's.
  • A tile is "sorted" when it is face-up in the column matching its number.
  • First player to sort all of their tiles wins the race!
